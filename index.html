1. Connection Setup

To connect two tabs:

  1. Tab A (Host): Click "Create Offer". Copy the SDP.
  2. Tab B (Peer): Paste SDP into "Remote SDP", click "Join (Create Answer)". Copy the Answer SDP.
  3. Tab A (Host): Paste Answer SDP into "Remote SDP", click "Receive Answer".
